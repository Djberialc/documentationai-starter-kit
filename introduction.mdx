## Quick Start

Get up and running in minutes. Follow these steps to create your first documentation page.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Create a New Page" icon="plus">
    Navigate to the dashboard and click "New Page". Choose the `introduction.mdx` template.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Add Frontmatter" icon="file-text">
    
````yaml
---
title: Your Page Title
description: Brief page description
---
````

    Paste this at the top of your file.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Insert Components" icon="puzzle">
    Use `<Columns>` for layouts or `<CodeGroup>` for multi-language examples.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Publish" icon="upload">
    Save and publish. Your page appears in the navigation instantly.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Callout kind="tip">
  Start small: Focus on one section per page. Use H2 headings and build from there.
</Callout>
