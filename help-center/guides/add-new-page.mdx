---
title: Adding a New Documentation Page
description: Quick guide to add a page in Вячеслав Пономарев's docs.
---

## Steps

<Steps>
  <Step title="Go to Project">
    Navigate to your selected project in the dashboard.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Click 'Add Page'">
    Find and click the "Add Page" button in the sidebar.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Edit Content">
    Enter your title and write your content in the editor.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Save">
    Press "Save" to publish the page. It will appear in your project's navigation immediately.
  </Step>
</Steps>
