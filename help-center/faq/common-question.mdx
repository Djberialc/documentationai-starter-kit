## Getting Started

To begin organizing your project documentation:

1. Visit the dashboard at `https://vyacheslav-ponomarev.com/docs`.
2. Click "Create New Project".
3. Name your project and provide a brief description.
4. Start adding pages using the "Add Page" button.

## Tips

- Use folders to group related docs.
- Update the sidebar for better navigation.
- Share your docs by inviting team members.
